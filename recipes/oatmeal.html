Oatmeal

Oatmeal: The perfect minimal effort breakfast!

Let's face it, we've all had our fair share of rushed mornings lately. With the go go go of modern life the last thing I want to do is spend a lot of time preparing breakfast. I have places to be, preferably outside of the kitchen! This is where oatmeal has come to save the day for me. The following recipe can be considered a baseline template to add to and modify to your tastes. I personally love strawberry but maybe you are more of a blueberry person? Maybe you prefer a dairy free meal to start the day? All modifications are welcome!

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 Cup Rolled Oats
  2. 1/2 Cup Milk
  3. 1/4 Cup Greek Yogurt
  4. 1/2 TBSP Chia Seed
  5. 1/2 TBSP Hulled Hempseed
  6. 1 tsp Cinnamon
  7. 1/2 TBSP Syrup
  8. 4 sliced Strawberries
