Hummus

The Hummus Among Us

In my years as a cook my favorite dishes to prepare have changed often but the one constant hit is absolutely Hummus. It is a perfect all season dish that can function as a side dish, salad topping, or sandwich/wrap protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/2 Cups of Chickpeas
  • 1/4 Cup Lemon Juice
  • 1 Clove Garlic, peeled and chopped.
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Tahini
  • 1 tsp ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp ground Berbere
  • 1/4 tsp ground Cayenne
  • 1 TBSP Olive Oil
  • Half bundle of fresh Parsley, de-stimmed, & Chop

Steps

  1. Boil Chickpeas until soft to touch.
  2. Drain Chickpeas set aside to cool with Ice cubes.
  3. Combine Lemon Juice, Garlic, Salt, & Parsley in food processor.
  4. Add Tahini, Cumin, Berbere, & Cayenne to food processor & blend.
  5. Combine Chickpeas and Hummus Mixture in the food processor.
  6. Add 1/4 to 1/2 Cup Water & blend.
  7. Add Olive Oil and Cumin while blending until the Hummus reaches a soft and creamy texture.
  8. Taste test and adjust Salt levels to your preference.
  9. Enjoy!
